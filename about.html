About

Nurse Practioner Dr. Rhonda Hettinger's medical practice with a focus on heart disease prevention and family health care.

As the owner of Personalized Prevention: Your Family & Heart Care, Dr. Hettinger delivers a unique focus on prevention and provides an educational approach to promoting women’s health. “The early detection of heart disease through aggressive screening and treatment is necessary and the most effective means of preventing a heart attack; women will live longer.”
